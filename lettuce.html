Storage Tips

To keep your arugula fresh for longer:

  • Store unwashed arugula in a plastic bag with a paper towel to absorb moisture
  • Keep in the refrigerator's crisper drawer
  • For best flavor and nutrient retention, use within 2-3 days of purchase
  • Wash only before using, as moisture speeds up spoilage
